1)Install a Siri GUI

2)Get iPhone 4S keys

3)Convert sessionValidationData with sr.rb (put it in your Desktop)

These are the commands:

cd ~/Desktop
sudo ruby sr.rb

4)Put the siriactivator.dylib and siriactivator.plist in /Library/MobileSubstrate/DynamicLibraries

5)Put your iPhone 4S keys in com.andev.siriactivator.plist

6)Put com.andev.siriactivator.plist in /var/mobile/Library/Preferences

7) Change your com.apple.assistantd.plist (System/Library/LaunchDaemons/)like that in the photo : http://cl.ly/2i3n2X3L3k372g2N0y07

8)Reboot;

9)Have a good Siri.
